Naini Lake

A picturesque freshwater lake in the heart of Nainital...

Naini Lake is one of the most famous attractions of Nainital. Surrounded by lush hills, it offers boating, scenic views, and a serene atmosphere.

Snow View Point

A popular spot offering panoramic views of the Himalayas...

Read More

Snow View Point gives visitors breathtaking views of snow-clad Himalayan peaks. Accessible via a cable car ride, it’s a must-visit for nature lovers.

Mall Road

The main shopping hub of Nainital, buzzing with cafes...

Read More

Mall Road is a lively stretch filled with shops, cafes, and local handicrafts. Perfect for shopping, dining, and evening strolls.

Tiffin Top

Also known as Dorothy’s Seat, a hilltop picnic spot...

Read More

Tiffin Top is a popular picnic spot that offers panoramic views of Nainital town and surrounding hills. Great for trekking and photography.
